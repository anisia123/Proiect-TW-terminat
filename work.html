Specimens & Remains

This row threads together the quiet presence of death—its symbols and its stillness. Each piece whispers of endings, but also of what lingers beyond.

Threaded Silence

A solitary spider poised in stillness, evoking quiet tension and delicate control.

Background Music

A surreal head spins vinyl at its core—where sound becomes memory, and silence bleeds emotion.

Echo of Mortality

A stylized skull on green, symbolizing life’s fragility with a pop-art twist.

Identity in motions

Here, form fractures and flutters. These works explore the shifting nature of self—how we wear emotion, how we break, how we rebuild.

Flutter in Crimson

A butterfly mid-flight, splashed with red—freedom tinged with intensity.

Fragmented Self

Abstract face in monochrome geometry—identity fractured, yet composed.

Pulse & Memory

Anatomical heart framed by bold stripes—raw emotion meets graphic clarity.

Nature Interrupted

A softer rhythm flows through this row—organic, fluid, and alive. These pieces speak of growth, drift, and the quiet persistence of nature.

Still Life in Bloom

A potted plant basking in window light—growth, calm, and domestic serenity.

Drift & Depth

Stylized fish in blue—fluid motion and underwater mystery.
