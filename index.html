About Me

From the age of four to sixteen, I immersed myself in art classes that shaped both my technique and my perspective. Over the years, curiosity became my compass—guiding me to explore, question, and reinterpret the world around me. I often find beauty in unexpected places: in textures, in silence, in fragments. My work is a reflection of that search—an ongoing dialogue between emotion, observation, and form.
